ABOUT

Saveetha University is proud to present our Fresh Harvest Market, a unique initiative that allows you to purchase vegetables that are freshly harvested right on our campus. Our commitment to sustainable agriculture and healthy living drives us to provide you with the freshest produce possible. By purchasing from us, you are not only supporting our agricultural efforts but also ensuring that you and your family enjoy nutritious, farm-to-table vegetables. Join us in promoting a healthier community and a greener environment.
